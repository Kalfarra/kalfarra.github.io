About Me

```

Welcome to my personal website. I’m a graduate student at UofT working on a number of things, primarily commutative algebra and algebraic geometry. In particular, I have taken a keen interest in toric geometry and its connections to multigraded commutative algebra.

I am currently under the supervision of Hunter Spink for the MSc degree at UofT.
```
